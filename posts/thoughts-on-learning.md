## 學習是一輩子的事

我一直相信，學習不應該止於學校。真正的學習發生在我們對世界保持好奇的每一個瞬間。

## 建立系統，而非目標

與其設定「今年要讀 50 本書」這樣的目標，不如建立每天閱讀 30 分鐘的習慣。系統比目標更可靠，因為它們不依賴意志力。

## 教是最好的學

When you try to explain something to others, you discover the gaps in your own understanding. Writing this blog is, in a way, my method of learning in public.

## 擁抱不確定性

> 「我知道的越多，越發現自己不知道的更多。」——蘇格拉底

Learning to be comfortable with not knowing is perhaps the most important skill of all. It keeps us humble, curious, and open to new ideas.

## 實踐大於理論

Read less, do more. The best way to learn programming is to build things. The best way to learn writing is to write. Start before you feel ready.
